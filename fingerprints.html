During the early days of the internet, HTTPS was expensive, so it wasn't very common to come across a website with one. However, when the Internet became more prevalent in doing integral tasks, HTTPS was a necesity. HTTPS makes sure that only the client and the server gets access to the data. One thing that helped in making HTTPS commonplace was Certificate Authorities (CA). Websites can visit CA to create a secure connections after they prove their identity.
